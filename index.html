Review of "The Force Awakens"

Okay, I lied. I haven't seen the movie yet. In fact, I have very little interest in the movie or in Star Wars itself. But, I have done some research relating to the movie that you might find interesting as a Star Wars fan.

The Reviews

Rotten Tomatoes

Rotten Tomatoes gave it excellent reviews. In fact, it gave it a 93% which is an A! The average rating was an 8.2/10. It even got better reviews than The Revenant and that was a very good movie.

Rolling Stone

Rolling Stone gave this movie 3.5/4 stars! That's incredible! Maybe I should take you to go see with all of these credible movie review sites giving it such great reviews.

Roger Ebert

Roger Ebert also gave this movie 3.5/4 stars. Okay, I'm sold. I'll take you to go see it. It sounds like a classic I can't afford to miss out on. The question is, when should we go?
